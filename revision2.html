Artificial intelligence is becoming a reality thanks to the development of machine learning. Big companies are starting to take notice and finally hardware is able to run these taxing and complex algorithms. Developers are finding amazing and creative ways to apply deep learning. Running these in the cloud is costly, suffers from latency, and network connectivity issues. All that remains is a simple way to implement these applications on the device.

How It Works:

  • Idea

    Design your mobile application, build and train your neural network!

  • Optimize

    Send us your Neural Network, and we will optimize your Neural Network to work on any device with our simple API.

  • Deploy

    Release your app to the public. Every time your app is downloaded, our optimized Network will be distributed to your user's device; because the Neural Network runs on the device itself, you save on server costs.




Advantages Over Cloud

  • Increased performance

  • Decreased development time

  • Minimized data costs

  • Functions on every device

  • No network required
